Enjoy the experience of nature

Visit and enjoy our eco-center every week-end !

Our eco-center in the nature is opened every week-end so that you can enjoy being in the countryside, in a place where you can have fun and relax with activities for all the family !

playground, swimming pool, relaxing area, reading area.
Eco-restaurant with fresh ecological product homecooked.
Workshops to learn and discover at any age about permaculture, crops, ceramic, painting, or to practice meditation, yoga, or Tai-chi !
Visits with explainations about our garden, plant nursery, compost and waste treatment area.
Stage with live music and a diversity of shows (see our programation)
Camping zone
And much more ! !

We are opened for the visitors from saturday to sunday, from 9am to 6pm

    ECOFESTIVAL para la familia (FESTICAMP)

    FESTICAMP es un festival que busca acercar a las familias

    chiclayanas (o lambayecanas) a marcas e iniciativas eco

    festival daremos a conocer las prácticas que aplicamos en la

    producción de nuestros cultivos, productos, artículos y

    servicios y en nuestro día a día en general para llevar un estilo

    de vida más sustentable.

    Contaremos con espacios para la feria, talleres, música en vivo,

    malabares, juegos y zona de campamento.
